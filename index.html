About us

Welcome to Ashwini Lab

Non-Invasive Oncology Diagnosis

Background: Current Oncology Diagnosis methods are expensive, invasive, harmful (radioactive, X-Rays, powerful magnets, etc.,) and in most of the cases the disease discovery is delayed.

Our Vision: Provide cost-effective, efficient, and accurate non-invasive cancer diagnosis through AI/ML driven Smartphone App and API integrations with other Clinical Software.

Our Innovation: Dr. Chaganti, founder, is a Doctorate in Condensed Matter Physics with specialization in Quantum Dots and Nano particles. He did Bachelor’s in Biomedical Engineering. His independent research interlinks Physics, Biomedical, and Ayurvedic Concepts on which he experimented, analyzed, and concluded various evidence-based results on the relation among heartbeats, voice, and other sounds from the human body to various medical conditions in the body including heart condition, blood pressure, diabetic, liver, brain, cancer, etc. Similarly, the electromagnetic waves produced by parts of the human body and their functions are inter-related.

Oncology Diagnosis: Based on his research work, he developed smartphone app on Vital Signs to detect blood pressure interlinking to subject heartbeats. Now we are advancing our innovation with non-invasive and cost-effective smartphone app to diagnose cancer in humans by collecting one or more of heartbeats, voice, respiration, heatmapping, and other electro-magnetic waves produced by the body. We would apply advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI)/Machine Learning (ML) algorithms augmented with physics, biomedical, and ayurvedic medical sciences to produce accurate diagnosis for cancer. We also have plans for data cloud storage and integrations with third-party systems for two-way data sync through API with data augmentation and extensions to our diagnosis services. Along with Dr. Chaganti, the Co-founders of our company also have extensive IT, financial, AI/ML, and healthcare/pharma/clinical trial experience.
