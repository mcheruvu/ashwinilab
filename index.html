About us

Welcome to Ashwini Lab

Non-Invasive Oncology Diagnosis and Treatment

Background: Current Oncology Diagnosis methods are expensive, invasive, harmful (radioactive, X-Rays, powerful magnets, etc.,) and in most of the cases the disease discovery is delayed. Oncology Drug/Treatment is equally expensive, painful, and 80% of last 10-year period phase III drug trials unable to conclude clinically meaningful improvement in overall survival from cancer.

Our Vision: To provide cost-effective, efficient, accurate, and non-invasive diagnosis through Smartphone Apps/Medical Devices, and Drug/Treatment with no side-effects to cancer patients.

Our Innovation: Dr. Chaganti, founder, is a Doctorate in Condensed Matter Physics with specialization in Quantum Dots and Nano particles. He did Bachelor’s in Biomedical Engineering. His independent research interlinks Physics, Biomedical, and Ayurvedic Concepts on which he experimented, analyzed, and concluded various evidence-based results on the relation among heartbeats, voice, and other sounds from the human body to various medical conditions in the body including heart condition, blood pressure, diabetic, liver, brain, cancer, etc. Similarly, the electromagnetic waves produced by parts of the human body and their functions are inter-related.

Oncology Diagnosis: Based on his research work, he developed smartphone app on Vital Signs to detect blood pressure interlinking to subject heartbeats. Now we are advancing our innovation with non-invasive and cost-effective smartphone app to diagnose cancer in humans by collecting heartbeats, voice, respiration, heatmapping, and other electro-magnetic waves produced by the body. We would apply advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI)/Machine Learning (ML) algorithms augmented with physics, biomedical, and ayurvedic sciences to produce accurate diagnosis for cancer. We also have plans for data cloud storage and integrations with third-party systems for two-way data sync through API with data augmentation and extensions to our diagnosis services. We would build medical equipment with advanced sensors to make our diagnosis predictions more accurate for medical professionals and the industry. The Co-founders our company also have extensive IT, financial, AI/ML, and healthcare/pharma/clinical trial experience.

Oncology Drug/Treatment: Gaining sources from Ayurvedic medical sciences, we have found the leads to drug/treatment for cancer patients and with that we are ready to initiate the drug research, discover the drug roots, get lab testing of the drug compositions as soon as we acquire meaningful funds. Our goal is to make the drug ready for clinical trials on human beings within three to five years timeframe. We have identified the subject-matter-experts in medical and ayurvedic domains to be in our advisory board to guide us in cross-validation of our drug/treatment findings and in grade improvements.
