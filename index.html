Y-HealthSigns App Features

Health Signs

Check your health/vital signs such as heartrate, blood pressure, respiratory rate, and temperature. This App stores the data, updates your condition, gives your physical location, and can send the data to your physician if you choose to do so.

Cardiac Signs

Monitor your cardiac function, detect rhythm of your heartbeats, strength and timing of the electrical impulses passing through each part of your heart, analyzes heart damage, enlarged heart, heart attack and to screen for heart diseases.

Diabetes Signs

Diabetes patients determine their blood glucose levels by prick a finger, test strip with blood drops in a glucometer. Our app monitors your blood sugars and detects the stage of your diabetes in a non-invasive fashion.

Lung Signs

Analyze your lung capacity i.e., how much air your lungs can hold and help diagnose COPD (Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease), Bronchiectasis, tuberculosis, and emphysema due to smoking or lung cancer.

Liver Signs

Understand your liver disorders that can cause fatigue, loss of appetite, jaundice etc. Also, Hepatitis viruses that cause liver damage, analyze autoimmune hepatitis, chronic alcohol abuse, and liver cancer.

Reports

This App securely stores data, displays your health/vital reports, scheduled alarms, and can also send the reports to your physician. Much cheaper transaction fee to keep your health check effortable.
